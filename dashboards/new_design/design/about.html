About Nemesis Labs

Nemesis Labs was founded with a singular mission: to bring absolute transparency to the blockchain ecosystem. As decentralized finance scales, the opacity of cross-chain routing, mixers, and bridging protocols has provided cover for illicit activities.

Our proprietary Omni-Chain Tracing Engine utilizes advanced graph theoretical algorithms and machine learning to cut through the noise. By ingesting petabytes of transaction data across 18+ blockchains and mapping it against our 1.2 billion-node ontology, we attribute wallets to real-world entities in milliseconds.

We serve top-tier law enforcement agencies, cyber insurance firms, and institutional custodians, providing them with the intelligence needed to intercept funds, identify threat actors, and comply with global regulatory frameworks.

Our Vision

A secure, transparent, and accountable Web3 ecosystem where institutional capital can flow without regulatory or counterparty risk.

Our Technology

Powered by the GBIO (Global Blockchain Intelligence Ontology) Framework, capable of evaluating 50,000 transactions per second natively.
